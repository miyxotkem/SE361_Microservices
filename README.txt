The E-Learning System is a new digital platform designed to replace the current manual, paper-based, and fragmented classroom processes for course registration, content delivery, and student assessment. The system acts as a centralized hub for educational interactions, initially serving internal students and faculty. As the platform evolves over future releases, it is expected to integrate with external academic databases, global payment gateways for premium certifications, and third-party video conferencing APIs to support fully remote, synchronous learning environments.
